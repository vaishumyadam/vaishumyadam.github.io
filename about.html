About Me

This is what I do.

Hello! My name is Vaishnavi Myadam (Vaishu). I am currently studying Statistics and Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign with an expected graduation date of May 2022. In the future, my goals are to continue my education in graduate school all the while gaining professional and academic experiences.

Whether it is through rigorous classes, interesting projects, research, professional and leadership experiences, I aim to explore the world of data through machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science. This website, through various posts, presents my data science and machine learning projects such as sentiment analysis and image captioning. In addition, it provides an overview of the natural language processing research I conducted at the School of Information Sciences.

At Illinois, I am heavily involved in the student community as part of the Content x Diversity Team for the Reflections | Projections Tech Conference, mentor to incoming CS students through SAIL, and a mentor to first year honors students through the Honors James Scholar Mentorship Program. Outside of classes, I enjoy baking, volunteering in my communities, and reading any and all novels by Malcolm Gladwell.
