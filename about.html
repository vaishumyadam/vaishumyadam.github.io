About Me

This is what I do.

Hello! My name is Vaishnavi Myadam (Vaishu). I am currently working as a Senior Associate in Trading Technology at Sunrise Futures (a quantitative trading firm).

Prior to starting at Sunrise Futures, I graduated with a B.S. in Statistics and Computer Science (with a minor in math) from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in May 2022.

When I'm not involved in academics, work, and volunteering, you will find me exploring Chicago, following Bob Ross tutorials, organizing every corner of my home, and reading Malcolm Gladwell books.
